Swimming is my favorite sport. It is basically
the only sport I am good at because I don't have
good cordination. Swimming also doesn't hurt my back
like other sports would. I have a back injury and
swimming actually relieves some of the pain.

My favorite things about swimming
is how easy the sport is for me.
For some people swimming can be
really hard, but it just comes
naturally for me. I also love the
teams I swim with. Everyone is
supportive and its like a mini family.

The events I usually swim
in a meet are the 100 free
and 50 free. I swim these
because I am very good at the
stroke. I can swim the 100 under
a minute and the 50 in under 27
seconds. I am happy with these
times because I qualify for states.
